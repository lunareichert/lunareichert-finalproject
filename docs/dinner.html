breakfast recommendations

best coffee and breakfast in austin

Patrizi's

Pasta in a food truck may sound unusual but Patrizi's knocks it out of the park and for a solid price. While the pasta is great, get some appetizers too. If you go on a weekend, get there early to avoid long lines.

Elizabeth Street Cafe

A great choice for a lively and falvorful dinner in Austin, Elizabeth Street cafe serves up the best elevated Viatnamese food.Their spicy chili wontons and spring rolls are must order appetizers.

Check out the website
Uchi

An Austin classic that has blwon up and opened several locations in Texas and beyond, Uchi is on every list for a reason. Their service and food is top tier and worth it for a special celebration night. Their happy hour is also an excellent deal.

Check out the website
Hestia

A recent Michelin-star winner for a reason, Hestia is a smoke-forward sister retsurnat of Emmer and Rye. A great spot for special occasions, their pork chop is a must try.

Check out the website
Este

Suerte's seafood inspired twin, Este is a must try for Mexican. I love the fish tacos, but any dish is excellent. It is also worth it to make a stop in Bar Toti across from Este while waiting for your table.

Check out the website
Bufalina

Try Bufaline if you are looking for a no-frills, more upscale pizza resturant. The place is small and does not take reserevation so be prepared to wait, but the pizza is worth it.

Check out the website
Birdie's

This Austin resturant is make fine-dining food in a homey resturant. Their menu is constantly rotating and is small, so try as many things as possible while you are there!

Check out the website
Discada

This food truck only sells one entree, the discada tacos, but it is worth it. They are the perfect blend of sweet, savory and spicy. All toppings are a must and order a side of elote as well to accompany the food.

Check out the website
Lin Asian Bar

Located on West 6th, Lin is serving up delcious dim sum in a small house-turned-resturant. Try some of their famous soup dumplings for an appetizer and go for one of their entrees, like Kung Pao chicken as well.
