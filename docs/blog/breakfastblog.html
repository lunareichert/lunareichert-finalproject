What makes a good breakfast spot?

When I am looking for the best breakfast, there are a few things I am looking for. First of all, I am looking at their beverages. As an avid coffee drinker, it is essential that they not only serve quality coffee but also have some fun and unique flavor options available. I typically stick with plain and simple coffee, but when I am going out for breakfast, I want to spend a little extra to get a fun drink.

The second thing I am looking for is a solid breakfast sandwich. I enjoy savory breakfasts much more than sweet breakfasts. While I always like to make sure the menu is balanced and has options for both, I always opt for savory. The breakfast sandwich is the perfect choice to evaluate the quality of a restaurant in one, complete bite.
