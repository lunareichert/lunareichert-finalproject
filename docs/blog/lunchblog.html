To Austin

What makes a good lunch spot?

I love lunch, especially on a weekend when you get to enjoy it more and it's less of a check off a list. Similar to breakfast, I think a sandwich is one of the best lunch foods. However, I usually prefer a sandwich on a busy day and something a bit more complex on a weekend.

Location is important for lunch stops. Some of my favorite spots are located in popular Austin areas like South Congress, which make them easy and delicious stops during a busy weekend day. Joann’s Fine Foods and Homeslice are two classic and fun stops for a weekend afternoon on SoCo. I love Homeslice for the walk-up window, which makes it a perfect Austin lunch spot.

I also look at ambiance and design. All-Day Pizza and Homeslice both have walk-up windows with extensive outdoor seating, which makes it a fast and fun stop to enjoy Austin weather. Fresa’s is also a wonderful spot for the Austin vibe, with Tex-Mex and a large outdoor patio good for big groups.
