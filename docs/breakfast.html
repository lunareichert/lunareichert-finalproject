To Austin

breakfast recommendations

best coffee and breakfast in austin

Paperboy

Whenever your family comes to town, take them to Paperboy. The small but lively resturant is a great brunch spot for the food, drinks, and ambiance. While its always booked and busy, its worth the extra effort to plan ahead to make a stop here.

Check out the website
Desnudo

Some of the best coffee and the most kind staff in Austin. Their miso latte is a must-order when visiting either of their locations. I recommend ordering pick up, as they usually have a long (but fast moving) line.

Check out the website
Two Hands

The location and the food makes it perfect for when guests are in town and wanting to visit South Congress. I recommmend the b.e.k. roll and a brown sugar syrup latte when visiting, but any choice is a good one. Reservations are recommended here.

Check out the website
Sour Duck Market

Close to UT campus, Odd Duck's sister resturant is the perfect casual breakfast or brunch spot. Their breakfast sandwich is one of the best and they also have a large assortment of pasteries as well. Easy spot for on the go, as reservations are not necessary.

Check out the website
Veracruz All Natural

Whenever in Austin, breakfast tacos are a must. Veracruz has some of the best and the location is right near Desnudo's downtown location so you can grab one of each while you are in the area!

Check out the website
Swedish Hill

While arguably overpirced, Swedish Hills pastries and overall ambiance makes it an Austin morning favorite. Their cafe selection and food is all very high quality with a large variety of options for customers to chose from.

Check out the website
Civil Goat

Another favorite coffee spot, Civil Goat has both drinks and food. Their acai bowls and avocado toast are nice light breakfast options to accompany their coffee. Another great option for a more on the go stop!

Check out the website
Bird Bird Biscuit

Although more on the heavy side, Bird Bird Biscuits are delicous and decdanat. As a native Goergian, I approve their biscuits! Located in east Austin, it is not far from campus and downtown making it a convient option for you.
