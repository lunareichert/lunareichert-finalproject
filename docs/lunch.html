To Austin

lunch recommendations

best midday stops in austin

Foodheads

Located close to UT, foodheads offers comforting soups and sandwiches everyday except Sundays. I always opt for a half sandwich and a cup of soup to get the best of both worlds. My favorite is Lami!

Fresas

Perfect for larger groups, Fresa's is a reat Tex-Mex stop for all the Austin vibes and a large outdoor patio. A great option for large groups, they serve great chicken and tacos and is worth a stop on a sunny spring day.

Allday Pizza

Allday is serving up some slightly elevated traiditional slices in three locations across Austin. The cacio e pepe pizza and the vodka slice are the best, but I recommend going with a firend and trying as many as you can!

Local Foods

A Sunday to-go classic, the crunchy chicken sandwich is a lifesaving sandiwhc that puts Local Foods on the list. Filled with chips, poached chiekn, and a delciosu green stop it makes it worth the slightly high prices.

Kome

If you are looking for well-priced and high quality sushi, kome is the place to go. Their rolls are fresh, but still fun. My favorite is the spooky roll and the sunshine, but any of them are a solid choice.

Homeslice

An Austin classic, stop by their window on South Congress for a slice of one of their pies. My favorite order is actually the italaina sandwich. Nonetheless, Homeslice is a classic Austin pizza spot.

Lou's

If you are looking for a sanwich, burger or salad look no further. The green chile burger is the top choice on the menu, but they also serve hearty salad for a more healthy option. The Lou's green sauce is a must with any fry or sandwich.

Joann's Fine Foods

Another South Congress spot, Joanns Fine Foods has healthy Tex-Mex offerings that make it a great stop of your Austin days. The oudoor patio is my favorite spot to eat at for the Austin ambiance in addition to the food and location.
